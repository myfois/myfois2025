How to Read an FOI Response

A short guide to reading responses carefully and keeping the original wording in context.

FOI responses can be precise, limited, or conditional. The wording matters, especially where a public authority says it does not hold information.

Keep the original nearby

A formatted version is useful, but the original response should remain available so readers can verify the wording.
