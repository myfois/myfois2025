University of East London Requested: 29 Sep 2025

Employment Tribunal Claims, Settlements, NDAs, and Legal Costs

Request

I am making this request under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

For the period from 1 January 2020 until 29 September 2025, please provide the following information:

1. Employment Tribunal Claims

For each year: The total number of employment tribunal claims lodged against the university.

For each claim:

The year;

The broad category of complaint (e.g. unfair dismissal, discrimination, whistleblowing, other);

The outcome, categorised as:

upheld at tribunal,

dismissed at tribunal,

settled before hearing,

withdrawn,

struck out/other.

2. Settlements & NDAs

The number of employment-related settlements reached (whether or not the case proceeded to tribunal).

Of those settlements, how many involved the use of a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) or confidentiality clause.

The aggregate (total) financial value of such settlements (please provide in rounded totals; I am not seeking individual case details).

If available, please provide this figure broken down by year.

3. Legal Costs

The total external legal costs incurred by the university in relation to employment tribunal claims or settlements in the period (e.g. solicitor and barrister fees).

If available, please provide this figure broken down by financial year.

I am not requesting personal data and am content with aggregated or anonymised information.

If providing the information in full would exceed the cost/time limit, please prioritise providing the totals per year (questions 1, 2 and 3 in aggregate).
