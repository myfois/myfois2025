SCOPE "Academic" and "Non-academic" staff are both on standard (non-atypical) contracts and always sum to the whole-institution total. "Atypical academic" staff (e.g. guest lecturers, hourly-paid teaching) are reported as a separate population by HESA – they are not part of the total above and should not be added to it.

Reading these numbers

Non-academic (professional services) staff outnumber academic staff. In 2024/25, UEL reported 1,095 academic staff against 1,110 non-academic staff on standard contracts, plus a further 430 academic-atypical staff (guest/hourly-paid teaching) reported separately.
CAVEAT This table has no subject or department dimension, so occupation counts cannot be linked to specific academic schools or the enrolment growth seen in the Student Origin Explorer. Figures are rounded to the nearest 5 by HESA's disclosure control; category sums may not exactly match published totals.
