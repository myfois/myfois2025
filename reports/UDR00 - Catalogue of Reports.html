UDR00 — Catalogue of Reports

Index of all UEL Data Reports (UDR01–UDR10) in this folder. University of East London (UKPRN 10007144).

Click any report title below to open it. Each report is a self-contained HTML file with its exact data sources listed in its own footer. For fuller detail on methodology and how to recreate each report, see Report_Catalogue_Detailed.md in the Catalogues folder alongside this one; for this same list in plain-text form, see Report_Catalogue_Simple.md.
National Student Survey (NSS)
01

NSS Explorer

Interactive explorer of raw National Student Survey results for UEL, 2023–2026, Registering & Teaching datasets, all subjects and themes.

02

NSS Analysis Report

Narrative analysis of UEL's NSS results 2023–2026 — highlights, lows, trends, and the 2025→2026 change in particular.

03

Subject Size, Growth and NSS Satisfaction Explorer

Cross-references HESA subject enrolment size/growth against NSS satisfaction scores, by subject.

Enrolment & subject mix
04

Enrolment Explorer (Subject, HESA Table 49)

UEL student enrolment broken down by subject of study, 2021/22–2024/25.

05

International Student Explorer (HESA Table 28)

Non-UK domiciled student enrolment by country, 2014/15–2024/25.

Superseded in scope by UDR06 — kept as a standalone, narrower view
06

Student Origin Explorer (UK and Intl Domicile)

Combined UK (Table 59) and international (Table 28) domicile explorer — countries, UK regions, and undergrad/postgrad × UK/Intl breakdown, 2014/15–2024/25.

Staff
07

Staff Number Explorer

UEL staff numbers by type, occupation and mode of employment, 2014/15–2024/25.

Data-quality cross-checks
08

Domicile Sources Cross-Check (Table 39 vs 28/49/59)

Cross-validates HESA Table 39 against Tables 28, 49 and 59 for the years they overlap (2016/17–2018/19), to confirm the domicile/subject data sources agree.

09

Enrolment Reconciliation (Subject vs Domicile)

Reconciles subject-based totals (Table 49) against domicile-based totals (Tables 28+59) across all 11 years, down to CAH3 course level.

Qualifiers & outcomes
10

Qualifiers, Degree Classification and Non-Continuation Explorer

HESA Table 16 qualifiers by level and first-degree classification, plus HESA T3/T5 and OfS Condition B3 continuation/completion data for "Other undergraduate" awards.
